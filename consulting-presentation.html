The Cost of Figuring It Out Alone

Common mistakes in coworking launches — each costs months and serious money

Wrong product mix for the format
What works at 5,000 sqm fails at 500. Too many hot desks, wrong office sizes, missing meeting rooms — 6+ months of underperformance before you reconfigure.
Underestimating OPEX
Personnel, cleaning, utilities, maintenance, marketing — the real cost structure is invisible until month 3. Cash burns faster than expected.
Building before pre-selling
Opening day with 0% occupancy. No pipeline, no anchor tenants, no corporate deals signed. Every empty month is pure loss.
No market research for the area
Pricing blind, positioning wrong, competitors unknown. Demand assumptions based on gut feel instead of data. Repricing later destroys brand trust.
